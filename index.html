Our Doctor

Dr. Chuah Kee Seng

Dr. Chuah pursued his higher education and obtained his Bachelor Of Dental Surgery from University of Malaya in 2005. He was then continue to study his postgraduate degree in the University of Malaya and graduated in the year 2008. Dr. Chuah subsequently obtained his postgraduate diploma in Advance Aesthetic & Restorative Dentistry at Uk, London. He is a diplomate of the AARD (UK).
He served as a dental officer on top of the attachment in the Oral-Surgery, Oral Medicine & Periodontology, Orthodontics, Restorative Department at Faculty of Dentistry, University of Malaya from 2005 till 2008.
Dr. Chuah's aim is to provide painless dentistry to his patient with the most reasonable fees. He is also very passionate especially towards children and elderly besides working in all aspects of dental care.

Dr. Chuah enthusiasm in learning never stop. He enriched himself by attending courses on the latest technology in Dental field.
He is currently a member of Malaysia Dental Association and is fully registered with the Malaysia Dental Council.
